Hello, welcome to my website!


Hi! I have completed my Bachelor’s in Statistics and did a research internship for three months in the Health Systems & Population Studies Division (HSPSD) at icddr,b. Currently, I am teaching “Categorical Data Analysis” course and conducting “Categorical Data Analysis”, “Time Series”, and Biostatistics lab classes to a group of young statisticians in the Statistics Department, primarily fourth-year undergraduates.

Born in the bucolic countryside of Magura, Khulna, Bangladesh. I spent the larger part of my childhood in Nohata, a small village nearby Mohammadpur, where I attended at Nohata Rani Patit Paboni High School before moving to Dhaka to pursue my college education at BAF Shaheen College Dhaka . I later obtained my bachelor’s degree in Statistics from Government Titumir College, University of Dhaka.

To know more about me, please visit the other corners of this website!
